The Methodological Gap in Modeling Mobility

Deterministic LCP

  • Basic Least Cost Path (LCP)
  • Assume perfect landscape knowledge
  • Single optimized route
  • Ignores uncertainty & exploratory behavior (Howey, 2011)

Circuit Theory

  • Probabilistic flow across landscape
  • Evaluates environmental traversal
  • Accounts for travel uncertainty
  • Robust alternative (McRae et al., 2008)
